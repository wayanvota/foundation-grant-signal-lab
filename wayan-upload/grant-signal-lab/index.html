        Responsible AI guardrails

        What this tool will not do.

        The score is a triage signal, not a moral judgment on the applicant or a funding decision. The tool structures evidence, risk, and next diligence so a grantmaker can defend the call to a board.

        No automated awards

        It will not make the funding decision or replace trustee judgment.

        No mission-worthiness score

        It scores visible grant-review signal, not the value of the people or mission.

        No substitute for program officers

        It will not replace a human read of leadership, context, relationships, and trust.

        No hidden verification

        It reads only what you paste today. It does not verify claims against 990s or public sources yet.

        Why funders should care

        A grants manager should reduce false confidence.

        The point is not to automate grantmaking. The point is to make judgment visible: fit, risk, missing evidence, next diligence, and board language that can survive scrutiny.

        The model is asked for evidence, risks, and next actions, not a summary and not praise. A grants platform should spend more intelligence where judgment matters and less where the task is repeatable.
